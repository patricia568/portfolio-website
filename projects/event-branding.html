The process

Colour palette

Each theme of the design has a different colour palette. Each palette has variations of the same colour. This helps in distinguishing a clear separation of one theme from the other. The overall colour theme for the identity of the CoE also works well when incorporated with the other colours. The theme is muted, however the colours stand out when paired together.

Typography

Arial Narrow was chosen for the headings as it is slim enough to fit lengthier titles, but also complimentary to the body copy.  Arial Regular was chosen for the body copy as it is clear and legible on both web and print. Both fonts were chosen for accessibility to users and the team.
