Intergalactic mother and child

Year completed

2022

Tools used

Adobe Illustrator

The humpback whale is one of the most compassionate mammals in the ocean. Not only do they defend their own kind, but they care for the well-being of other ocean dwellers by protecting them when predators attack. Courageously enough, they even protect humans. This piece shows the love a mother whale has for her child, and the love that she is willing to share with others, across every galaxy.
